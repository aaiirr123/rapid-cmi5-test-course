:::stepContent{title="CIA Triad"}
What are the three core principles of the CIA Triad in cybersecurity?

**Answer**

* **Confidentiality** – Ensuring data is only accessible to authorized users
* **Integrity** – Ensuring data cannot be altered without detection
* **Availability** – Ensuring systems and data are accessible when needed
:::

:::stepContent{title="Hashing"}
What is a cryptographic hash and what is it used for?

**Answer**

A **hash function** converts data into a fixed-length string of characters.

Common uses:

* Password storage
* Data integrity verification
* Digital signatures

Example algorithms:

* SHA-256
* SHA-3
* BLAKE2
:::

:::stepContent{title="Encryption"}
What is the difference between **symmetric** and **asymmetric** encryption?
**Answer**

Symmetric encryption is **faster**, while asymmetric encryption
is commonly used for **key exchange and authentication**.
